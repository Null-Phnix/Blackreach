## Quick Customization Checklist

For EACH job application:
1. ✅ Company name (3-5 times throughout)
2. ✅ Specific job title
3. ✅ One detail from job description (tech stack, responsibility, or company mission)
4. ✅ Connect Blackreach feature to their needs
5. ✅ Explain WHY you're excited (be specific, not generic)

**Time per application:** 3-5 minutes if you use these templates
