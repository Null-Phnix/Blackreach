# AI Browser Competitive Analysis (2026)

## What is SigmaOS?

**SigmaOS** is a productivity-focused browser (YC-backed, raised ~$2M) with AI features, workspaces, and split-screen.

**The Reality:**
- **Mac-only** (cuts potential users in half immediately)
- **No public user numbers** (likely <50k users - very small)
- **Mixed reviews:** "Great design, horrible bugs, not worth switching yet"
- **Not a real threat** - too small, too buggy, Mac-only

**Verdict:** SigmaOS is NOT your main competition. It's a niche productivity tool, not a true AI browser.

---

## Your REAL Competition

### Tier 1: Direct AI Browser Competitors (Your Main Threats)

#### 1. **Perplexity Comet** 🔥 BIGGEST THREAT
- **Users:** 1 million+ (the market leader)
- **What it does:** Autonomous browsing - you say "research flight prices to Dubai" and it opens tabs, reads sites, compiles results
- **Strength:** Perplexity brand, already has huge user base
- **Weakness:** Locked to Perplexity's AI only, no customization
- **Your advantage:** You let users pick ANY LLM (OpenAI, Claude, Grok, local models)

---

#### 2. **ChatGPT Atlas** 🔥 STRONG THREAT
- **Users:** <5% of ChatGPT users tried it (~few hundred thousand)
- **What it does:** ChatGPT integrated into browser, AI sidebar for research/writing
- **Strength:** OpenAI brand, ChatGPT ecosystem lock-in
- **Weakness:** Mac-only, ChatGPT-only, no real automation (just chat)
- **Your advantage:** Cross-platform + real browser automation (not just prompts)

---

#### 3. **Dia** (formerly Arc) 🔥 STRONG THREAT
- **Users:** Invite-only beta (maybe 10-50k early users)
- **What it does:** AI-first browser with "Skills" - auto-fill jobs, distraction-free reading, custom shortcuts
- **Strength:** $610M Arc acquisition momentum, beautiful UX
- **Weakness:** Invite-only, locked to their AI, still early
- **Your advantage:** Open NOW, multi-LLM support, proven automation

---

### Tier 2: Indirect Threats (Bigger Players Adding AI)

#### 4. **Microsoft Edge Copilot**
- **Users:** 200M+ (already installed on Windows)
- **What it does:** Copilot chatbot in sidebar, reads tabs, summarizes pages
- **Strength:** Pre-installed on every Windows PC, Microsoft ecosystem
- **Weakness:** Not truly "agentic" (doesn't automate tasks for you)
- **Your advantage:** Real automation, cross-platform, not Microsoft-locked

---

#### 5. **Brave Leo**
- **Users:** 60M+ Brave users (privacy-focused)
- **What it does:** AI chat sidebar, private (doesn't send data to servers)
- **Strength:** Privacy-first, local model options, huge user base
- **Weakness:** Basic chat only, no automation or agent capabilities
- **Your advantage:** Full agent automation, multi-tab parallel ops

---

### Tier 3: Emerging Players (Not Real Threats Yet)

- **Sigma** (agentic browser) - No user numbers, very early
- **Fellou.ai** (autonomous browser) - New, unknown
- **SigmaOS** (productivity browser) - <50k users, Mac-only, buggy
